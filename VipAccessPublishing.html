RocBoi Quez Signs Non-Exclusive Publishing Deal with DJ Vip of VipAccessPublishing

In an era where independent artists are rewriting the rules of the music industry, RocBoi Quez stands as a prime example of disciplined artistry, strategic independence, and cultural authenticity. The Atlanta-based hip-hop recording artist has officially entered a non-exclusive publishing agreement with DJ Vip, founder of VipAccessPublishing—marking a defining moment for underground and independent hip-hop.

Who Is DJ Vip and Why This Matters

DJ Vip is widely respected as the official DJ of the late Nipsey Hussle, a role that places him among a rare class of cultural custodians within hip-hop. His work through VipAccessPublishing reflects a deep commitment to ownership, legacy, and artist-first economics.

RocBoi Quez: Built from the Ground Up

RocBoi Quez’s career has been forged through consistency, catalog development, and a refusal to compromise authenticity. His music speaks directly to hustlers, independent thinkers, and fans seeking truth beyond mainstream narratives.

Crossing Over into Chopped & Screwed

By expanding into chopped and screwed formats, RocBoi Quez has strengthened his cultural footprint while extending the life and emotional depth of his catalog. His work honors Southern hip-hop traditions while introducing them to new audiences.

The Power of a Non-Exclusive Publishing Deal

This non-exclusive structure allows RocBoi Quez to retain ownership while benefiting from professional publishing administration and strategic positioning. It is a forward-thinking model that reflects where independent music is headed.

Motivating the Underground

RocBoi Quez’s journey sends a message to underground artists everywhere: ownership matters, catalogs matter, and sustainability outweighs short-term exposure. His alignment with VipAccessPublishing is a roadmap—not a shortcut.

Legacy Over Hype

This partnership is about longevity, royalties, and cultural preservation. It reflects a belief that real success in hip-hop is measured not by moments, but by legacy.

RocBoi Quez is not chasing the industry. The industry is finally catching up to him.
