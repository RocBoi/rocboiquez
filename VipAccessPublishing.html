RocBoi Quez Signs Non-Exclusive Publishing Deal with DJ Vip of VipAccessPublishing

Atlanta hip-hop artist RocBoi Quez has officially entered a non-exclusive publishing agreement with DJ Vip of VipAccessPublishing, marking a strategic alignment centered on ownership, longevity, and cultural preservation.

VipAccessPublishing & DJ Vip’s Cultural Authority

VipAccessPublishing, led by DJ Vip—best known as the official DJ of the late Nipsey Hussle—has established itself as an artist-first publishing entity. The partnership reinforces independent catalog control and professional royalty administration.

RocBoi Quez: Independent Momentum

Through disciplined releases, direct audience engagement, and global digital reach, RocBoi Quez continues to build a sustainable career outside major-label dependency. His official platform at rocboiquez.com serves as the central hub for announcements and exclusive content.

Chopped Not Slopped: Cultural Positioning

RocBoi Quez’s expansion into chopped not slopped pays homage to Southern hip-hop tradition while positioning his catalog for DJs, tastemakers, and underground audiences worldwide.

Why Non-Exclusive Publishing Matters

Non-exclusive publishing structures preserve ownership while enabling professional collection and administration. This approach allows artists to scale strategically without surrendering long-term leverage.

Looking Ahead

RocBoi Quez’s upcoming release, Get Out Your Feelings (Chopped Not Slopped), is scheduled for a February rollout via Bentley Records.io, with an official release date to be announced.
