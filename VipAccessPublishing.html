RocBoi Quez Signs Non-Exclusive Publishing Deal with DJ Vip of VipAccessPublishing

Atlanta hip-hop artist RocBoi Quez has officially entered a non-exclusive publishing agreement with DJ Vip of VipAccessPublishing, marking a strategic alignment rooted in ownership, longevity, and cultural preservation.

VipAccessPublishing & DJ Vip’s Cultural Authority

VipAccessPublishing, led by DJ Vip—best known as the official DJ of the late Nipsey Hussle—has become synonymous with artist-first publishing strategy. The partnership reinforces values of independence and catalog control.

RocBoi Quez: Proven Independent Momentum

Through disciplined releases, direct-to-fan monetization, and global digital reach, RocBoi Quez has built a sustainable career outside major-label dependency. His official catalog, exclusive drops, and DJ-ready releases are available directly at rocboiquez.com .

Chopped & Screwed: Cultural Expansion

RocBoi Quez’s crossover into chopped and screwed honors Southern hip-hop tradition while introducing his sound to a wider underground audience. This expansion extends the lifecycle and emotional resonance of his catalog.

Why Non-Exclusive Publishing Wins

Unlike traditional publishing contracts, non-exclusive structures preserve ownership while enabling professional royalty administration and global collection. This allows RocBoi Quez to scale without surrendering leverage.

Call to the Underground

This deal signals to underground artists worldwide: build your catalog, protect your masters, and align with partners—not gatekeepers. RocBoi Quez exemplifies how independent artists can thrive on their own terms.
