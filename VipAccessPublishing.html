RocBoi Quez Signs Non-Exclusive Publishing Deal with DJ Vip of VipAccessPublishing

In an era where independent artists are rewriting the rules of the music industry, RocBoi Quez stands as a prime example of disciplined artistry, strategic independence, and cultural authenticity. The Atlanta-based hip-hop recording artist has officially entered a non-exclusive publishing agreement with DJ Vip, founder of VipAccessPublishing—marking a defining moment for underground and independent hip-hop.

Who Is DJ Vip and Why This Matters

DJ Vip is widely respected as the official DJ of the late Nipsey Hussle, a role that places him among a rare class of cultural custodians within hip-hop. His work through VipAccessPublishing reflects a deep commitment to ownership, legacy, and artist-first economics.

RocBoi Quez: Built from the Ground Up

RocBoi Quez’s career has been forged through consistency, catalog development, and a refusal to compromise authenticity. His growing body of work—available directly through rocboiquez.com—demonstrates how independent artists can thrive outside traditional gatekeeping systems.

Crossing Over into Chopped & Screwed

By expanding into chopped and screwed formats, RocBoi Quez has strengthened his cultural footprint while extending the life and emotional depth of his catalog. His approach respects Southern hip-hop lineage while evolving it for a modern audience.

The Power of a Non-Exclusive Publishing Deal

This non-exclusive structure allows RocBoi Quez to retain ownership while benefiting from professional publishing administration and strategic positioning—an increasingly dominant model for sustainable careers.

Motivating the Underground

RocBoi Quez’s journey is a signal to underground artists worldwide: control your masters, respect your catalog, and build leverage. His partnership with VipAccessPublishing represents alignment, not dependency.

Legacy Over Hype

This collaboration prioritizes longevity, royalty flow, and cultural preservation. It is about building something that lasts—artistically and financially.

RocBoi Quez is not chasing the industry. The industry is responding to his momentum.
