RocBoi Quez

An underground Atlanta hip-hop legend, RocBoi Quez has been crafting raw, authentic southern music since his early days in the streets of Atlanta.

Known for unapologetic storytelling and innovative flows, he has become a voice for the city’s next generation of hip-hop.

The Purple Edition

The Purple Edition is a chopped-not-slopped album experience, blending smooth southern beats with hard-hitting lyricism.

It highlights RocBoi Quez’s versatility, his deep connection to Atlanta’s musical roots, and his ability to push the boundaries of contemporary hip-hop.
